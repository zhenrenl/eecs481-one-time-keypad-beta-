HOME SCREEN LAYOUT
Column 0: An ordered list of map IDs
Column 1: (optional): A preview of symbols in each map
Column 2-5: The 19 keypad symbols of the current map
Column 6: A list of options or a list of recommended words

INSTRUCTIONS
Press NumLock ('Switch') to go to the next map.
Hold Numlock ('Switch') and select one of 6 highlighted
buttons to jump to the corresponding map.
Press / ('CapsLock') to turn CapsLock on and off.
(Note: The following four occur if the list
of options is present in Column 6)
Press Home ('About') to get contact information
Press Tab ('Tutorial') to view this basic tutorial
Press Mail ('Profiles') to create, modify,
and switch to user profiles
Press Calculator ('Exit') to exit the app
